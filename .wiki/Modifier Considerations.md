# Modifier Considerations

## Guard Bell
The player may be able to "damage boost" through an otherwise difficult challenge by sacrificing hitpoints.

## Gold Rush
All levels with multiple Goals are treated as if in Collectathon mode; players will need to collect ALL Goal Tiles before Bouldo will allow them to proceed.
- This is because there is canonically only one Bouldo; it makes sense lore-wise that he'd want all the gems collected as well, anyway.
  - "Bring me the seven Chaos Emeralds!"

Make sure players can backtrack so that they can go back if they missed anything they need.

## Perfection / Pacifism
If you have really precise level design, it might be worth loosening it up a little so these modes are easier.

## New Game Plus
The player will start with every upgrade unlocked. Currently, this includes:
- Shuriken
- Sword
- Bow
- Double Jump Scroll
- Climbing Claws

This may change in the future to be determined by flags in the pack itself.

## Play As Elenn
Elenn has vastly altered physics;
- Much more slippery.
- Way faster maximum speeds.
- Longer Sword dash.
- Less powerful Shuriken throws, but are affected by movement speed.

In practice, she can do almost everything Ensy can, but it may be beneficial to make adjustments to cater to Elenn's physics.

## Invulnerability
Players can simply walk through all damage sources; the only things that can kill them are Pits and Suffocation, or Misses when combined with Perfection or Pacifism.
